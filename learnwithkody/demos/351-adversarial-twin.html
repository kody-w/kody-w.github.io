Build an adversarial review that can show its receipts.

Run two review lenses independently, record claims against exact evidence, resolve disagreement, and keep the final decision human.

1 · Scope the evidence

Same source set, different lenses

Name what both reviewers could inspect. Do not paste secrets; this page keeps data in memory but cannot make sensitive code safe to share.

2 · Work two lenses independently

Isolation before comparison

Finish and attest each pass before unlocking Compare. A complete claim needs a topic, criticism, exact location, exact quote, severity, and a stronger comparator.

3 · Record disagreements

Do not average two opinions. Capture both positions, then name the evidence and resolution that decides between them.

4 · Pass the human judge gate

No auto-grading

The app can detect blanks. It cannot tell whether a quote proves a claim, whether an alternative is better, or whether the verdict is wise.

Judge the evidence chain, not the cruelty of the persona. “Be right” is the gate; “be cruel” is only the pressure test.

5 · Check, seal, and export

Markdown packet
  • ○Review target, history scope, and evidence boundary are named.
  • ○Lens A has at least one claim, and every nonempty claim is evidence-complete.
  • ○Lens B has at least one claim, and every nonempty claim is evidence-complete.
  • ○Both independent-pass attestations are checked.
  • ○The lenses were compared after both independent passes.
  • ○Disagreements are resolved, or none remains.
  • ○The human judge supplied criteria, verdict, rationale, and attestation.
