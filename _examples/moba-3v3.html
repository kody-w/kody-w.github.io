What this is

Six heroes drafted from a 12-hero roster — assassins, carries, tanks, mages, supports — drop onto a three-lane MOBA map with jungle, river dragon, and two bases. Creep waves clash every 30 seconds. Heroes last-hit for gold, recall when low, gank vulnerable lanes. Dragon respawns with a team-wide damage buff. Towers fall in order; the ancient becomes vulnerable only after 3+ enemy towers go down. First nexus down wins; 10-minute timer falls back to most-towers-killed.

Why this is mind-blowing

Test runs produce real MOBA stories: an assassin snowballs to 9-0-1 by mid-game, the dragon flips a teamfight, three towers fall in 90 seconds, "NEXUS UNDER ATTACK" fires, the defender's last stand fails — final nexus crack at lap 9:04. K/D/A scoreboards, gold leads, ace banners. The ten-minute matches feel like LCS clips compressed.
